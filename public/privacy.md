# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025

## Overview

Promptyy is a collection of creative writing tools and utilities developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### What We Don't Collect
- **No personal information** - We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or other personally identifiable information
- **No accounts required** - You can use our tools without creating an account
- **No data storage** - We do not store your writing, prompts, or creative content on our servers

### What We Automatically Collect
- **Usage analytics** - Basic information about how our tools are used to improve the service
- **Technical data** - Browser type, device information, and general location data for analytics
- **Performance metrics** - Response times and error reports to improve functionality
